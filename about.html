Hello visitor,

My name is Ronan Smith, and I am an undergraduate student, studying BSc Computer Science at Heriot-Watt University. I am currently in my fourth and final year of studies and hope to graduate in 2018. I have a great interest for computing and technology and recently became a STEM Ambassador, meaning I have recieved a full disclosure for working with children and I will be able to take part in voluntary activities to promote the learning of STEM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths). I am a positive, hardworking individual and outside of university I like to keep myself busy. In my spare time I like to keep fit by going to the gym and playing 5-a-side football. I enjoy socialising with friends, visiting new places and experiencing new things. At home I enjoy playing the PS4 and watching TV series; some of my favourites are Game of Thrones, American Horror Story, Silicon Valley and Mr Robot. Currently I am working part-time in a local pub but my goal is to find a career in the technology industry, and I am currently looking for and applying for full time work.

I am a keen learner, and some of my favourite areas of study in computer science so far have been artificial intelligence, database management and web design. I aim to use this website or development blog to show off some of my achievements and to describe my work in more detail than would be possible on a CV. On my current timetable, you will find me in university on Mondays and Wednesday to Friday. Usually, I will work from home on a Tuesday. In my time in the university building outside of classes you are likely to find me in the Linux lab (Earl Mountbatten builing, second floor) or the lunch hall!
