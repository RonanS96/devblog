About Me

Ronan James Smith



Hello visitor,

My name is Ronan Smith, and I have just completed my final year studying BSc Computer Science at Heriot-Watt University. I graduated in June 2018 with Honours of the First-Class. I have a great interest for computing and technology and I am signed up to the STEM Ambassador scheme, meaning I have recieved a full disclosure for working with children and I am able to take part in voluntary activities to promote the learning of STEM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths) in schools. I am a positive, hardworking individual and outside of university I like to keep myself busy. In my spare time I like to keep fit by going to the gym and playing 5-a-side football. I enjoy socialising with friends, visiting new places and experiencing new things. At home I enjoy playing the PS4 and watching TV series; some of my current favourites are Westworld, American Horror Story, Silicon Valley and Mr Robot. At the moment I am enjoying a summer break before I begin my full-time working career in August as a "Graduate Developer".

I am a keen learner and although I may be finished education, my plan is to never stop learning! Some of my favourite areas of study in computer science so far have been artificial intelligence, database management and web development. I aim to use this website or development blog to show off some of my achievements and to describe my work in more detail than would be possible on a CV.
