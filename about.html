About Me

Hello visitor,

My name is Ronan Smith, and I am an undergraduate student, studying BSc Computer Science at Heriot-Watt University. I have just completed my third year of studies and hope to graduate in 2018. I have a passion for computing and technology and enjoy the challenge of my course. I am a positive, hardworking individual and outside of university I like to keep myself busy. In my spare time I like to keep fit by going to the gym and playing 5-a-side football. I enjoy socialising with friends, visiting new places and experiencing new situations in general. Currently I am working part-time in a local pub but I am interested in developing my working career in a more technology based area.

I am currently on a summer break from university and after having no luck in gaining relevant work through an internship or summer placement, I have decided that I will use my summer to work on some personal projects, one of which is this 'development blog'. I plan to use this blog to help make people aware of the work I have been doing in and out of university and have included lots of information about coursework projects I have undertaken. Due to strict university rules about sharing coursework with other students I will not be making my source code available on this site, but can happily show evidence of my work if required.
