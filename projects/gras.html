G.R.A.S

The Gambit Resource Allocation System



For our third year group project, we were put into groups of 7. To begin with my group had four computer science students (including me), one information systems student, one computer systems student and one student from Electronic and Computer Engineering. We were given the task from a real science and technology company Leidos to create a complex system that could be used to intelligently assign staff to projects. With 3 dropouts throughout the year our group was reduced to only 4 people by the end; Myself (Computer Science), James Hanson (Computer Science), Angus Cameron (Electrical and Computer Engineering) and Cameron McKinnon (Information Systems).

We named ourselves Gambit Software and our solution was called the G.R.A.S (Gambit Resource Allocation System). I will be writing up a document to provide a full description of the G.R.A.S as soon as I get the chance and it will be available here as soon as it is ready.
