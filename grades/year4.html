HONS Project: Deliverable 1

Year 4, Semester 1
Grade: B (68%)
An Online Tool to Choose a Programming Language. The first part of my honours project involved writing a literature review, some background research on similar products, designing the product I plan on creating, carrying out some project planning and carrying out a survey. This was worth 15% of my overall grade for the project.

Biologically Inspired Computation

Year 4, Semester 1
Grade: B (66%)
This module gave me an insight into some of the software that is around that is based on different biological systems. This is an alternative to traditional ways of developing software and gave me interesting experience in working with large datasets. The first coursework for this module involved building an artificial neural network and an evolutionary algorithm.
