DISSERTATION COMPLETED

An Online tool to Choose a Programming Language, 23rd April, 2018

Today I submitted the final version of my dissertation, written for my fourth year Honours Project An Online tool to Choose a Programming Language. The focus of the dissertion was creating a website that could help programmers find a language that suits their needs. Research carried out for the project involved finding out what the most popular programming languages were and what makes different languages useful in different contexts.
